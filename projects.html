Garfield Onsite Research Study - USA - San Jose or Santa Clara

The purpose of this study is to gather video data of you interacting with your pet (cat/dog) in a large room with controlled lighting. 
If you agree to enroll and are selected, you will be asked to hang out with your pet in a moderated 1-hour session, where you and your pet will be video recorded walking around, interacting with you, and/or playing with toys. 

This is an onsite study taking place at our premises in San Jose or Santa Clara.

To participate in the study you must be 18 years of age or older, have a pet that is at least 1 year old and vaccinated and be willing and able to participate in the study procedures described in the consent form.

The purpose of this study is to gather data from different homes throughout the Bay Area. The collected data will be used to improve and train technological services and applications.

This study will take place in your home which must be located in the South Bay Area. To participate all household members must be 18 years or older, with no children under 18 living in the home.
