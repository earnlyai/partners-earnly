Frequently Asked Questions

Payments

First-Time Contributor: Which steps take place before I get paid?

Once your KYC is approved, you’ll get instant access to your Earnly Partners Dashboard using the email you provided.

  1. Inside your dashboard:

    You’ll receive task updates and notifications, and can manage your payout settings at any time.

    • Choose your payment method: PayPal, Payoneer, Wise, or Bank Wire
    • Securely connect your account or enter your payout details
    • Payments are sent automatically
    • Set your schedule: weekly or monthly payouts
  2. No extra steps needed:

    No tax forms. No invoices. No manual requests.
    Just complete tasks → get paid.

    Note: All payouts are processed in USD. Ensure your selected payment method supports international transfers if you're outside the U.S.

How do I update my payment information?

You can update your banking or payment details at any time through your secure Partners Dashboard. Simply navigate to the Payment Settings section to modify your preferred payout method (PayPal, Payoneer, Wise, or bank wire).

Please note: For security and compliance reasons, changes to sensitive payment information — such as bank account or wire transfer details — may require additional identity verification before the update takes effect. This safeguard helps protect your account and ensures payouts are delivered accurately.

All pending payouts will reflect your most recently verified payment method. Please allow up to 24 hours for changes to be fully processed. Note that updates apply only to future payments and cannot be applied retroactively.

How much will I be paid?

Compensation is determined on a per-project basis and may vary depending on task complexity, estimated time commitment, geographic location, data sensitivity, and required expertise. Every opportunity is designed to reflect the value of your contribution and the specialized nature of the work.

All tasks on the Earnly platform are compensated at a minimum of $50 per assignment, with standard remote micro-tasks typically ranging between $50 and $300. For larger, high-impact projects — such as longitudinal user studies, multi-stage data annotation sprints, or on-site validation sessions — compensation can scale significantly, with select opportunities offering payouts of up to $2,000 or more.

Payment structure may be delivered as a fixed per-task rate, hourly compensation, or project-based milestone payments, and will always be clearly disclosed before you accept an assignment. Earnly is committed to fair, transparent, and competitive pay that recognizes the critical role partners play in advancing responsible AI systems.

When do I get paid?

For remote projects:

Once you complete a task, our quality review process begins immediately. You’ll receive your Quality Check results — and confirmation of compensation — within **24 to 48 hours** of submission. Payouts are then processed automatically based on your selected payment method and schedule (weekly or monthly).

For on-site studies:

Payment processing begins **immediately** after you successfully complete your session on-site. You’ll receive confirmation and payout according to your chosen payment method, typically within the same payout cycle.

What payment methods are available?

We support a range of secure and reliable payment methods to ensure you get paid quickly and conveniently. Available options include PayPal, Payoneer, Wise (formerly TransferWise), and international bank wire transfer. The available methods may vary slightly based on your country of residence, but all partners have access to at least one fast, low-fee digital payout option.
