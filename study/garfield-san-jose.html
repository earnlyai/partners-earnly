Garfield Onsite Research Study - San Jose or Santa Clara

Welcome and thank you for your interest in our study! 

We’re currently seeking pet owners to participate along with their dogs or cats in our onsite research study in San Jose or Santa Clara.

Image

What is the study about?

The purpose of this study is to gather video data of you interacting with your pet (cat/dog) in a large room with controlled lighting.  If you agree to enroll and are selected, you will be asked to hang out with your pet in a moderated 1-hour session, where you and your pet will be video recorded walking around, interacting with you, and/or playing with toys. 

 

Image

Who is eligible?

To participate you should meet the following qualifying criteria:

  • Be 18 years or older.
  • Be a resident of the United States.
  • Be able to manage your pet’s behavior during the session.
  • Your pet must be at least 1 year old and vaccinated.
  • Be able to visit our premises in San Jose or Santa Clara for scheduled onsite session.
  • Be able to communicate in English effectively and follow instructions from the study team.
Image

Where is the study taking place?

This is an onsite study taking place at our premises in San Jose or Santa Clara.

Image

How much is the compensation?

You will receive $120 USD for your successful participation.
As payment method, we offer PayPal, Gift Card, Check and Wire Transfer, according to your preference.

General Referral Bonus Program 
We offer a referral bonus of $20 USD for each participant you refer to the study who successfully completes the onsite activity session. 

How to refer someone: 
- Share this registration link with your friends and family 
- When registering, they should answer Referral to the question "How did you find out about this opportunity?" and provide your name and email address in the fields: First Name, Last Name and Email Address. 

Image

How long does it take?

It will take up to 1 hour to complete the study session.
